I built this site as a place to demonstrate my skills with HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript and related libraries like jQuery. The design is based on one I adapted from a template on Wix.com, a drag-and-drop editor. You can view the original design here. ...Read More

My initial goal was to rebuild my Wix site using purely the development skills I had at the time. As it has progressed, I have improved the experience by adding a fixed global navigation element, and by repositioning content to prioritize the needs of my users. I hope to continue to demonstrate good User Experience principles through my design as I learn new skills and attempt to incorporate them. Keep in mind, it is a work in progress. Enjoy! Less
